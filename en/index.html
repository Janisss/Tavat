About us

The company provides comprehensive services within the Slovak construction market. We realize your living from the very beginning to the complete delivery of the project.

Our goal is to satisfy the customer's needs in the construction, reconstruction and modification of buildings, public spaces, homes and flats. We approach each customer separately and try to meet all your requirements at the best prices.

We achieve customer satisfaction with high performance using up-to-date technology and high quality materials.

First, we will consult with you, then we will take care of the smooth running of the reconstruction, the construction of a new building or the construction work. To your satisfaction we will resolve any complaints.

Construction activity

The construction activity we are dealing with is mainly focused on the reconstruction of housing cores and interiors of flats and family houses. For our customers we provide the following types of crafts: tiling, masonry, wiring and plumbing. We will appreciate the work on the basis of a project or customer design - we will prepare a budget. All works are contracted and treated according to the applicable building law and the Commercial Code.
